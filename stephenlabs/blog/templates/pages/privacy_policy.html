Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

StephenLabs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect information when you visit or interact with our website, content, services, and communications.

By accessing or using StephenLabs, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

StephenLabs is a technology-focused platform providing educational content, tutorials, technical writing, and insights across software engineering, DevOps, blockchain, Web3, and related fields. StephenLabs operates globally and serves users worldwide.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services, improve content quality, and communicate effectively with our users.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address (e.g., newsletter subscription)
  • Name or username (if provided)
  • Information submitted through forms
  • Messages or inquiries sent via contact forms
  • Feedback or comments

Providing this information is optional, but some features may not be available without it.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URLs
  • Date and time of access

This information is collected for analytics, security, and performance optimization purposes.

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience, remember user preferences, analyze site traffic and performance, and enhance security. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Delivering newsletters and updates
  • Providing requested content or services
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Analyzing trends and usage patterns
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring platform security
  • Communicating important updates or changes
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Newsletter and Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • Your email address is stored securely
  • You will receive periodic updates, tutorials, and insights
  • Every email includes an unsubscribe link
  • You may unsubscribe at any time. Once unsubscribed, your email will no longer receive communications unless you re-subscribe.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent – when you voluntarily provide information
  • Legitimate interest – improving services and communication
  • Legal obligation – compliance with applicable laws

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including secure server infrastructure, access controls, authentication, encrypted communication, and limited access to personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time, subject to legal requirements.

8. Sharing of Information

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, users, or platform
  • With trusted service providers strictly for operational purposes

9. Third-Party Links

StephenLabs may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Children’s Privacy

StephenLabs is not intended for children under 7. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us immediately.

11. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

Requests can be made by contacting us directly.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
